About Us

Dr. SPM IIIT-Naya Raipur, established by the International Institute of Information Technology University Act, 2013 of the Government of Chhattisgarh, is a joint venture by Chhattisgarh State Government and National Thermal Power Corporation (NTPC). The institute is committed to pursue excellence in higher education, research and development in Information Technology (IT) and associated disciplines. It firmly believes in bestowing knowledge dissemination and imparting entrepreneurial skills to the students to enable them to address real world problems. iiit-nr campus tour

The three undergraduate programmes provided by iiitnr-curriculum are B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), B.Tech. in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), and B.Tech. in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence (DSAI). These credit-based programmes in our curriculum are designed to help students develop important skills like creativity and entrepreneurship. The dynamic structure of the programme responds to the changing demands of industry and academia. The Fractal Curriculum is followed at IIIT Naya Raipur.
